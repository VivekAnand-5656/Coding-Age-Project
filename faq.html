Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the mode of classes?

We offer both online and offline classes. You can choose the mode that fits your schedule best. Our online classes are conducted via Zoom and include live sessions, while offline classes are held at our Patna campus.

Q2: Is there a certificate provided?

Yes, we provide certification after successful completion of each course. The certificates are recognized and add value to your professional profile. You can download your certificate from our portal once the course is complete.

Q3: What is the fee structure?

The fees vary depending on the course. For instance, the Web Development course costs ₹30,000, while courses like Data Structures and Java Programming are priced at ₹25,000. You can find the detailed fee structure on our admission page, or you can contact our admission team for personalized queries.

Q4: Are there any discounts available?

Yes, we offer discounts during certain promotional periods, and special discounts are available for students who enroll in multiple courses. Keep an eye on our events page or sign up for our newsletter for the latest offers!

Q5: What support is available during the course?

We provide 24/7 online support for students via our community forum and chat support. Additionally, instructors are available for office hours where students can get one-on-one help. We also offer a dedicated technical support team to assist with any platform-related issues.

Q6: Can I switch batches after enrolling?

Yes, you can switch batches if you need to, depending on availability. We try to accommodate changes whenever possible. Please contact our support team to make the switch.

Q7: How can I apply for a job after completing a course?

Coding Age has strong industry connections and regularly organizes placement drives with top companies. After completing your course, you can attend our placement drives and get personalized career counseling from our team. We also offer job assistance for six months after course completion.

Q8: What are the prerequisites for the courses?

Each course has specific prerequisites. For example, for Web Development, you should have basic computer knowledge and an understanding of HTML. For advanced courses like Data Structures, prior knowledge of programming languages like C or C++ is recommended. You can check the specific course details on our course page.
